# Executive Brief on Today's LME Non-Ferrous Metals Market

## Key Findings

- **Aluminum:** Severe backwardation in the 3M spread, signaling tight physical supply. This suggests aluminum prices are likely to rise further in coming months.

- **Copper:** The 3M forward spread is deeply in contango, indicating a shift towards a more balanced market with ample near-term inventory. However, prompt spreads remain firm.

- **Zinc:** Zinc has moved into strong backwardation in the spot through 9M, pointing to growing physical tightness and higher prices ahead.

- **Nickel:** The nickel market remains firmly in contango across all forward curves. While inventories are ample, recent physical buying momentum is a flag to watch.

## Critical Shifts

- **Aluminum Structural Change:** A pronounced shift towards backwardation in the 3M spread implies an accelerating tightening of physical aluminum supply versus demand balances.

- **Copper Market Balance:** Although still in contango, the flattening 3M forward curve hints at a more balanced market with less pronounced near-term deficits vs. surpluses.

## Arbitrage and Physical Momentum

- **Aluminum & Zinc Absolute Arbitrage:** Given the severe backwardation, now is an opportunity window to arbitrage into aluminum physical positions or longer-dated paper ahead of further price gains.

- **Copper Spot-Prompt Spread Widening:** Although not at extreme levels, widening near-term prompt spreads vs. spot prices signal increasing physical demand and momentum to accumulate inventory.

- **Nickel Physical Buying Flag:** Despite contango, recent upticks in nickel physical buying by key consumer industries is a bullish flag warranting close monitoring for potential spread compression. 

## Strategic Implications

- **Aluminum & Zinc:** Strongly consider building positions now while backwardation and physical tightness flags are clearly illuminated.

- **Copper:** Maintain balanced book, leaning short the curve due to ample near-term inventory despite firm prompt spreads.

- **Nickel:** Monitor physical buying intensity for signs of tightening balances. Be ready to react if contango spread curves compress rapidly.
